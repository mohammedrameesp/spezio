About Spezio

Purpose-built for extended stays in Perinthalmanna.

Why We Built Spezio

Perinthalmanna welcomes thousands of visitors every month - families seeking medical care at renowned hospitals, business professionals, pilgrims visiting Thirumandhamkunnu temple, and guests attending family functions.

We saw a gap: visitors needed more than a hotel room. They needed a home - a place to cook their own meals, spread out comfortably, and stay affordably for days, weeks, or months.

Launched in December 2025, Spezio is purpose-designed for extended stays. Every apartment comes with a full kitchen, modern amenities, and the peace of being just 450 meters from the main highway - close to town, yet quiet enough for proper rest.

Close to Everything. Far from the Chaos.

Just 450 meters from the main highway, yet surrounded by greenery and calm. Hospitals, temples, bus stands - all within minutes. But when you return, you'll find peace and quiet waiting for you.

No traffic noise. No crowded corridors. Just your own private space to rest and recharge. That's the Spezio difference.
